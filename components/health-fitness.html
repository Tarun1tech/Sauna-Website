HEALTH & FITNESS

Health Tips

Our main themes look at promoting a healthy lifestyle in communities through the power of sports. Celebrating the SAUNA Sports Festival, SAUNA Community Run and SAUNA Kids Health Camp will highlight the role that sport plays as a factor in creating healthy communities/institutions at both physical and psychological levels.
Besides the physical activities, we use all our platforms to promote the need for natural foods and the use of natural medicines. Through its various health and fitness themes, we involve experts to educate people about the need to opt for natural foods because besides being free of synthetic or artificial ingredients or additives, their production does not cause environmental damage from pesticides and herbicides, and composted fertilizers help restore soil and are not as damaging to the environment as artificial fertilizers.

SAUNA COMMUNITY RUN

The SAUNA Community Run precedes the annual sports festival. This is open to all Ugandans and locals. It’s organized over 5km and 10km.

SAUNA SPORTS FESTIVAL

The SAUNA sports festival incorporates games in football, basketball, tennis, badminton, omweso, dodging (kwepena), volleyball, netball, and athletics (track and field).

SAUNA KIDS HEALTH CAMP

The SAUNA Kids Health Camp offers an opportunity for kids to play football and any other games of their choice.
